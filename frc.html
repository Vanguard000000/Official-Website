FRC Robotics — Chips #324

My path toward joining one of the most competitive robotics teams in the area.

Why FRC?

FIRST Robotics Competition is the pinnacle of high-school robotics — teams of students build industrial-size robots in six weeks to compete in a game that changes every year. I want to join Chips #324 because it combines everything I'm passionate about: engineering design, programming, teamwork, and competition under pressure.
